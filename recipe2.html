Swahili Pilau

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rice
  • 6 cups warm water (or meat broth)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 4 large onions
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 bulb of garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp ginger

Instructions:

  1. Boil the meat with salt and set aside.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown. Add meat, garlic, ginger, and salt. Stir and add water. Let it boil.
  3. Add spices and more water. Let it boil again.
  4. Add rice and meat broth. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked.
  5. Serve with a fresh salad and enjoy!
